Team Building & Collaboration: The Comic Strip Challenge
👥 Teams of 3–6🎯 Cross-functional📍 In-person
60 min

A high-energy creative workshop to break down silos and build real collaborative muscle. Teams co-create a 6-panel comic strip through structured storytelling, plot twists, and a mid-session panel swap. Deceptively playful — under the laughter, participants practice adaptability, rapid decision-making, and trust.

What’s covered
Rapid creative collaboration under pressure
Adapting to others’ ideas mid-flow
Non-verbal communication (visual-only panel)
Reflection & team storytelling
