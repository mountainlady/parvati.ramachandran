Team Building · Facilitation
Team Building & Collaboration: The Comic Strip Challenge
👥 Teams of 3–6 🎯 Cross-functional teams 📍 In-person
60 minutes

A high-energy creative workshop designed to break down silos and build real collaborative muscle — fast. Teams co-create a 6-panel comic strip through structured rounds of storytelling, plot twists, and a mid-session panel swap that forces them to build on each other's ideas without losing momentum. It's deceptively playful: under the laughter, participants are practising exactly what makes great product teams tick — adaptability, rapid decision-making, and trusting the people beside them.

What's covered
Rapid creative collaboration under time pressure
Adapting to others' ideas mid-flow
Non-verbal communication (visual-only panel)
Reflection & team storytelling
Materials: 📄 Facilitation guide 🎲 Story prompt cards 🖼 Panel templates
Product Leadership · Communication
Stakeholder Management & Communication for Product Managers
👥 5 Product Managers 🎯 Mid-to-senior PMs 📍 In-person · Interactive
4 hours

A deep-dive workshop for product managers who know their craft but want to level up how they work with the humans around them. Built around real-world scenarios and role-play, this session moves through the full arc of stakeholder influence — from understanding what drives different personalities using the DISC framework, to mapping power dynamics, asking better questions, and navigating the conversations no one wants to have. Participants leave with a personalised stakeholder map they can put to work the very next day.
